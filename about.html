Welcome! This exhibition was inspired by my love for digital archives and storytelling.

I thought to myself: If I was going to practice making interactive exhibitions for my portfolio, why not make it about a sport that I love?

I hope you enjoy exploring, and if you learned something, feel free to reach out and let me know!

"I DON'T BELIEVE IN LIMITS."
- Sergey Bubka
This simple digital exhibition was created by Michelle Lee (me!) using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It spans the history of pole vault: from its origins to modern day, exploring topics such as technique, equipment, and notable athletes. The project was inspired by my passion for pole vault, which I practiced at the Division I collegiate level at Columbia University while also obtaining my degree in Computer Science.

This work was inspired by digital exhibitions such as The Elders Project by Incite Institute, as well as other websites that I have worked with in the past. I hope to continue expanding my creativity and skills in digital storytelling in order to make even more immersive and complicated projects in the future.
